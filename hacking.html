What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking is when someone tests a system to find security problems, but with permission. Instead of causing damage, the goal is to fix weaknesses before real hackers find them.

Why is it important?

It helps protect personal data, prevent cyber attacks, and keep systems safe. Many companies hire ethical hackers to improve their security.

Simple example

Imagine checking a door to see if it is locked. Ethical hackers do the same thing, but with computer systems.
